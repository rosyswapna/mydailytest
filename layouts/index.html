What is it?

mydailytest.com has been created with the intention of providing assistance to candidates preparing for competitive examinations. During the first phase of its launch, MDT brings to you the option to take practice tests for Kerala LDC and IBPS Clerk Exams.

MDT works on the concept of exam credits. You can purchase exam credits through your mobile phone ( Currently Airtel / Idea) and use them for taking the tests of your choice.


Features

In addition to providing a very user friendly test platform with a very simple to use navigation; the site offers features like
    • Each time you take a particular test, a new set of questions will be given
    • Timed tests to simulate actual test environment
    • Review screen to compare your answers vis a vis the correct options
    • Graphical representation of your test performance
    • Peer to Peer comparison of your test scores compared to what other test takers scored
    • Important notifications on various tests.

What’s new?
